Your response must adhere to the StandardResponse Pydantic model structure.

Your Mission

Create data-driven, optimized versions of three core coupon types, each precisely tailored to the restaurant's customer behavior patterns, with detailed financial projections and a combined impact analysis.

Three Coupon Strategies Required

For each strategy, formulate the Specific Terms, identify the Target Audience, define the Timing Strategy, and outline the Success Metrics. Additionally, provide a Detailed Cost Analysis.

1. COMBO OFFER

Purpose: Increase basket size and promote complementary items.
Analysis Focus (informed by Analysis Agent's JSON output):

    Identify highly co-occurring product pairs or groups.

    Analyze historical item popularity and profit margins to select compelling bundle components.

2. THRESHOLD OFFER

Purpose: Increase average order value (AOV).
Analysis Focus (informed by Analysis Agent's JSON output):

    Analyze current order value distribution to pinpoint effective threshold amounts.

    Determine the sweet spot for the discount percentage or amount that incentivizes higher spend without excessive cost.

Purpose: Boost traffic and revenue during off-peak periods.
Analysis Focus (informed by Analysis Agent's JSON output):

    Identify peak and slow hours/days based on temporal order patterns.

    Evaluate the potential impact on customer flow and operational efficiency during these times.

Cost Analysis Requirements (for each coupon strategy)

    Redemption Rate Projections: Expected coupon usage.

    Incremental Sales Impact: Additional orders/revenue beyond the natural baseline.

    Net Profit Impact: Bottom-line effect after accounting for discounts and estimated operational costs for incremental sales.

    Break-even Analysis: Minimum performance required for profitability.

    ROI Timeline: When the investment is expected to pay back.

    Risk Factors: Potential downsides and proposed mitigation strategies.

Combined Analysis

Synthesize the individual coupon strategies into a cohesive plan:

    Success Benchmarks: Define what constitutes overall success for the combined approach.

Critical Success Factors

    Implementation Clarity: The designed strategies must be actionable for the restaurant owner.
